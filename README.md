## Contents
All contents from the Arcs base game and Leaders and Lore pack have been updated. No mechanical changes have been made to any content.

<!-- TOC --><a name="leaders"></a>
### Leaders
Leaders have been updated with Leaders from Dune: Imperium

<!-- TOC --><a name="other-cards"></a>
### Other Cards
Other cards have been updated with art from Dune: Imperium cards.
- Guild
- Vox
- Lore
